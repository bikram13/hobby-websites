## 2026-04-11 - US-004
- Fixed WCAG AA contrast violations across the site
- `text-amber-500` → `text-amber-700` in readability-checker easeColor() (amber-500 was ~1.9:1, amber-700 is ~4.7:1)
- `text-gray-500` → `text-gray-600` for xs stat labels on bg-gray-50 (gray-50 bg drops contrast just below 4.5:1 threshold)
- `text-gray-400` → `text-gray-600` for "Recommended Tools" heading in BaseLayout affiliate section
- `text-gray-400` → `text-gray-500` for all character-count labels, hints, and UI controls site-wide
- `text-gray-400` → `text-gray-600` for placeholder text on bg-gray-100 backgrounds (OG preview "No image URL provided")
- Files changed: `src/pages/writing/readability-checker.astro`, `src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro`, `src/pages/seo/open-graph-generator.astro`, `src/pages/seo/meta-tag-generator.astro`, `src/pages/seo/robots-txt-generator.astro`, `src/pages/seo/keyword-density-checker.astro`
- **Learnings:**
  - gray-400 (#9ca3af) on white fails WCAG AA at ~2.85:1 — always use at least gray-500 for functional text on white
  - gray-500 on bg-gray-50 is borderline failing (~4.37:1 vs 4.5:1 threshold) — bump to gray-600 when text is on tinted bg
  - amber-500 on white is severely low contrast (~1.9:1) — use amber-700 for readable warning/status colors
  - JS-injected className strings must be updated alongside HTML class attributes, or the JS will reset the fix on interaction
